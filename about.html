Who We Are

CCC Transport is an elite specialty shipping company based in Wisconsin, operating nationwide. We specialize in moving high-value, fragile, and irreplaceable cargo — paintings, bronze statues, antique furniture, vintage collectibles, and beyond.

Founded by John Corlan in , CCC has grown from a single van operation into a fleet of professional Mercedes Sprinter vans with drivers operating coast to coast. Our clients include auction houses, private collectors, galleries, and businesses who trust us with their most valuable items.

The CCC Difference

What sets us apart is simple: we treat every shipment as if it were our own. Every item is custom wrapped, padded, and secured. We build custom crates when needed. Our drivers are trained to handle high-value cargo with white-glove care.

The result? Zero damage claims . Not one. That track record is why UPS calls us for their specialty loads, why Mecum Auctions trusts us with million-dollar prototypes, and why 99% of our business comes from word of mouth.

Our Companies
